### SLIDE 2 — The Problem with "We Need AI"

Most universities jump to tools without knowing their starting point.

**56% of universities adopt isolated digital initiatives without strategic alignment**
(Bravo-Jaico et al., 2025)

Result:
- Pilot projects that never scale
- Faculty trained on tools nobody uses
- Technology investments with no measurable impact

The first step is NOT "what tool should we buy?"
The first step is **"where are we now?"**

### SLIDE 3 — Introducing the MTM Model

**Model for assessing the maturity level of digital transformation in higher education**
(Bravo-Jaico et al., 2025)

Based on real interviews with deans, IT directors, and school leaders in a Latin American university — adapted for Vietnamese HE.

**The model evaluates 8 dimensions** of your institution.
Each dimension is scored on **3 sub-criteria**:
1. **Tools & Processes** — Do the systems exist?
2. **Data** — Is data used for decisions?
3. **Culture** — Do people embrace it?

---

### SLIDE 4 — The 8 Dimensions

| # | Dimension | What it covers |
|---|-----------|----------------|
| 1 | Socio-cultural | Digital social responsibility, community impact |
| 2 | Teaching & Learning | LMS, digital pedagogy, learning analytics |
| 3 | Academic Management | Enrollment, student services, curriculum |
| 4 | Administrative Management | HR, finance, workflow automation |
| 5 | Research & Innovation | Digital research tools, patents, tech transfer |
| 6 | Digital Governance | Cybersecurity, data governance, digital strategy |
| 7 | Institutional Image | Digital branding, CRM, recruitment |
| 8 | University Extension | Community engagement, industry partnerships |

These 8 dimensions are a starting point. In the app, you can **hide** dimensions that don't apply to your context and **add custom ones** that matter to your institution.

---

### SLIDE 5 — The 3 Maturity Levels

For each sub-criterion, you rate your institution:

| Level | Label | What it looks like |
|---|---|---|
| **?** | I don't know | You lack visibility on this aspect |
| **1** | Beginner | Manual, no digital, no strategy |
| **2** | In Progress | Systems exist but underused, emerging culture |
| **3** | Continuous Improvement | Fully digital, data-driven, people-first |

**"I don't know" is a valid answer** — it reveals blind spots in your institutional awareness, which is itself a finding.

---

### SLIDE 6 — Example: Teaching & Learning

| Sub-criterion | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tools** | No LMS, manual course design | LMS exists but underused | Fully digital pedagogy with virtual environments |
| **Data** | No learning analytics | Basic usage stats | Analytics drive course design |
| **Culture** | Faculty unaware or resistant | Beginning to experiment | Actively innovate and share |

*Ask yourself: which column sounds like your institution?*

---

### SLIDE 7 — Example: Digital Governance

| Sub-criterion | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tools** | No cybersecurity, no policies | Basic security protocols | Full cybersecurity framework, Open Data |
| **Data** | Data in silos, no governance | Some governance initiatives | Centralized governance, privacy compliance |
| **Culture** | No digital strategy ownership | Emerging digital leadership | Transformational leadership |

**Warning**: If you score high on Teaching & Learning but low on Digital Governance → you are deploying AI without guardrails. This creates compliance risk.

### SLIDE 9 — AI Tool of the Day: Your Critical Advisor

By default, AI is a **yes-man**. It validates everything you say.

A change professional needs the opposite: someone who **challenges your blind spots**.

**Today you will learn to configure Gemini as a Red Team advisor:**
- You give it a role: *"You are a critical advisor for university transformation. Your job is to find flaws in my reasoning, stakeholders I'm forgetting, and risks I'm underestimating."*
- You submit your plan or your diagnostic
- The AI pushes back — hard

**This is the difference between using AI as a toy and using AI as a professional tool.**
